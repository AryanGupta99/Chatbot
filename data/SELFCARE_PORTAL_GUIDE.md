# SelfCare Portal Guide

## Overview
SelfCare Portal helps you manage your server password and Multi Factor Authentication. Enrollment is required to reset or unlock your account.

## Prerequisite
Install Google Authenticator on your mobile device.

## Enrollment Steps

### To log in and enroll:
1. Go to https://Selfcare.acecloudhosting.com
2. Log in with your server username and password
3. A barcode will appear
4. Open Google Authenticator and scan the barcode
   - If scanning does not work, choose manual entry and enter the numbers shown on the screen
5. Enter the one-time passcode generated by the app and select Next
6. Click "Click Here" to complete enrollment
7. Choose a security question, provide the answer, and select Next
8. Enrollment is complete

## Password Reset

### Forgot Password
- You must have enrolled at least once
- Go to the portal, click "Forgot your password?"
- Enter your username
- Follow the authentication prompts to reset your password

### Change Your Server Login Password
1. Log in to the SelfCare Portal
2. Go to Change Password
3. Enter your old password
4. Enter your new password and confirm it
   - Make sure it meets complexity requirements
5. Enter the CAPTCHA and continue
6. Select an authentication method
7. Re-enter your new password
8. Select Reset
