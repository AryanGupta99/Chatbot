Depending on the email service you use, follow the procedure listed below to set up email in QuickBooks for delivering invoices, reports, and other forms. Learn how to configure Gmail, Outlook, and other email clients in QuickBooks by following the below steps : 1. Open the QuickBooks application on the server. 2. Click on Open or restore an existing company to browse and open your project file . 3. From the menu bar, select Edit and click on Preferences. 4. Click on Send Forms and choose Web Mail under SEND E -MAIL USING field. 5. Click Add. 6. Enter the following details and click OK. Category Description E-mail ID Enter your email address. E-mail Provider Choose the e -mail provider for the E -mail address entered. Server Name Enter the server details provided by your hosting provider. For Example: smtp.outlook.com Port Enter the Port number. 7. A login window will appear. Enter your intuit account credentials and click Sign -in to complete the process.
