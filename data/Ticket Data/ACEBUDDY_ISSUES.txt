Ask:

"What printer problem?

Can't find printer

Printer offline

Print job stuck

Other"

Provide quick fix based on issue:

Printer offline:

Restart printer (power off for 10 seconds)

Restart your computer

Try printing again

6. SERVER DOWN / SLOWNESS / NOT RESPONDING / FREEZE

Ask interactive questions:

"Open Task Manager. What's your CPU percentage? (1-100)"

"What's your RAM percentage?"

"What's your Disk percentage?"

Diagnose based on numbers:

High CPU (>80%): Close unused applications, restart computer

High RAM (>85%): Restart your computer

High Disk (>90%): Delete old files, request disk upgrade

"Are you connected to the internet? (Yes/No)"

"Can you see an error message?"

Provide fixes based on answers:

No internet: Restart WiFi/modem
